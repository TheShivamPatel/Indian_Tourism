# Indian_Tourism
Indian tourism (app name) is a solution to making travelling easy in India by providing all personalize features in the app. 
This app helps tourists to get guides, find places and get accommodation that matches broadly with their preferences, by delivering as narrowed-down recommendations as possible.


### Future Scope
As travel and tourism is a trillion-dollar industry, digitalization can prosper this business. Travel app development has revolutionized the way people travel. These companies are hiring App development companies to create an app and flourish their business.


### Uniqueness
INDIAN TOURISM app… A single platform that provides 
you lots of useful features at one place .
“ DO ALL YOUR TRAVEL PLANNING IN ONE PLACE ”
Like : Use our planning tool to make your own plan instead of fixed packages , save your plans , and share and collaborate with your travel companions. With this platform we aim to help make you a better traveller , from travel planning , to booking , to take a trip. No matter what type trip you’re looking to take , the INDIAN TOURISM app makes planning it easy.
